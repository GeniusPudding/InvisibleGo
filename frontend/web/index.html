Rules
  • 9x9 Go board. You see only your own stones.
  • Empty-looking points may hide an opponent's stone.
  • You get up to 3 attempts per turn. All illegal moves look identical — the game never tells you why.
  • 3 illegal attempts auto-skip your turn (counts as a pass).
  • Two consecutive passes end the game; the full board is revealed and BLACK marks dead stones for WHITE to approve before scoring.
  • Win condition: Chinese area scoring with komi 4.5. Black's area = alive black stones + empty points bordered only by black; same for white. BLACK wins if black-area > white-area + 4.5 — on 9x9 that means BLACK needs at least 43 area points.
